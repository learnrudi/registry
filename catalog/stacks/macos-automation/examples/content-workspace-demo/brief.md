# Brief

## Working Title

Agent-assisted Mac workflow demo

## Goal

Show how a local automation stack can turn selected Mac context into a prepared
content workspace without exposing arbitrary AppleScript execution.

## Audience

Operators who use local files, Shortcuts, Reminders, and agent workflows to
produce business or content assets.

## Core Point

The Mac can become a controlled local action surface: agents can inspect context,
open the right workspace, trigger approved Shortcuts, create persistent next
steps, and notify the user.

## Constraints

- Keep destructive or state-changing actions behind explicit confirmation.
- Keep personal paths and account-specific defaults out of the public stack.
- Use private recipes for business-specific workflow details.
